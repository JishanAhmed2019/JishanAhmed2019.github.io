Jishan Ahmed

Data Science Assistant Professor & Machine Learning Scientist

I'm a Data Science Assistant Professor at Weber State University and a passionate Machine Learning Scientist. I hold a Ph.D. in Data Science (in progress) from Bowling Green State University, an M.A. in Mathematics from Ball State University, and advanced degrees from the University of Dhaka.

My mission is to develop cost-aware machine learning methodologies and open tools that democratize access to high-quality predictive algorithms.
